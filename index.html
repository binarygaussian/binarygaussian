Overview

Our method builds upon pre-trained 3D Gaussians by augmenting each Gaussian with multi-granularity, coarse-to-fine binary features. During training, we keep the opacity values learnable and optimize the binary features on rendered 2D projections via a progressive contrastive learning strategy. This strategy decomposes the complex segmentation task into a series of independent, localized sub-problems, thereby reducing training complexity and substantially improving the modelâ€™s capability to distinguish fine-grained categories.
